Hi I'm Mika
I'm a responsive designer and developer with a background in video, photography, music and other digital creative arts. I enjoy learning, solving problems, and creating beautiful and strange things.

I was born in Spokane, WA. But spent most of my childhood in Flagstaff, AZ and Palisade, CO. I did my undergraudate degree at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL. After graduating I spent 6 months in Portland interning with Miranda July before moving to the Twin Cities, where I have lived for almost 20 years.

My passions are foraging, permaculture, and spending time in nature and cuddling with my husband and our cat Kal. If I could I would probably just live in a remote cabin in nature with them both. It's amazing to be a conscious part of the universe also aware of itself.

Fun facts: I have a black belt in Shorin-Ryu Karate. I have completed a PDC with Geoff Lawton. I have a Master's Degree in Traditional Chinese Medicine. I have had art installations at the Soap Factory and Intermedia Arts.
